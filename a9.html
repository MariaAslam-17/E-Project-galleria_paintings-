Embers in Shadow

This evocative charcoal drawing explores the delicate tension between darkness and light. With rich tonal gradients and dramatic contrasts, the composition speaks to the hidden layers of the human condition. The smudged textures and soft edges evoke a dreamlike presence—part real, part remembered. It is a work that doesn't just depict a subject, but captures the atmosphere of a moment lost in time.
