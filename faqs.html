Frequently Asked Questions

What types of paintings do you offer?

We offer abstract, landscape, flower, nature, figurative, and religious paintings in various styles and mediums.

What type of paintings do you make?

I make acrylic, oil, and watercolor paintings. I love painting nature, people, and abstract art.

Can I order a custom painting?

Yes, you can! Just contact me and tell me what you want. I will make it for you.

What is the price range?

Most of my paintings are between 2,000 to 30,000 PKR, depending on the size and detail.

Do you deliver paintings?

Yes! I deliver all over Pakistan. Delivery charges may apply.

How long does it take to finish a painting?

It usually takes 3 to 10 days, depending on the size and work.

Are your paintings available internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Contact us for delivery options and charges for your country.
